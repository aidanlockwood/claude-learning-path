# Nutrition Plan for Iron Deficiency + Athletic Performance (Lactose-Free)

Given your BMI (~20.7, lean) and training demands as a competitive tennis player, here's a targeted approach:

## Priority: Iron-Rich Foods

**Heme iron (best absorbed, ~15-35% absorption):**
- Lean red meat (beef, lamb) – 3-4x/week
- Poultry (dark meat cuts have more iron than breast)
- Fish – sardines, tuna, salmon
- Eggs (moderate source)

**Non-heme iron (2-20% absorption, needs pairing—see below):**
- Legumes: lentils, chickpeas, black beans
- Tofu, tempeh, edamame
- Spinach, Swiss chard, kale
- Fortified cereals/oats
- Pumpkin seeds, cashews, quinoa

## Boost Absorption
- **Pair non-heme iron with Vitamin C**: citrus, bell peppers, strawberries, tomatoes
  - *Example: lentils + bell pepper stir-fry, or spinach salad + orange segments*
- **Avoid tea/coffee within 1 hour of iron-rich meals** (tannins inhibit absorption)
- **Cook in cast iron pans** when possible (adds trace iron)

## Managing Lactose Intolerance
- **Calcium interferes with iron absorption anyway**, so this is somewhat helpful—just ensure calcium from lactose-free sources at *different meals*:
  - Lactose-free milk/yogurt, hard aged cheeses (naturally low lactose), fortified plant milks (almond, oat, soy)
  - Leafy greens, sesame seeds/tahini, sardines with bones

## Sustaining Energy for Training

**Around training sessions:**
- **Pre-workout**: Oats + fruit, banana + nut butter
- **Post-workout**: Protein + carb combo — e.g., grilled chicken/fish + quinoa + vegetables, or a lactose-free protein shake with berries

**Daily energy support:**
- Complex carbs: brown rice, sweet potatoes, whole grains
- Healthy fats: avocado, olive oil, nuts/seeds
- Adequate hydration + electrolytes (important since sweat losses can also deplete iron slightly)

## Sample Day
- **Breakfast**: Fortified oats + strawberries + pumpkin seeds
- **Lunch**: Beef or lentil salad with bell peppers, spinach, citrus vinaigrette
- **Pre-training snack**: Banana + almond butter
- **Dinner**: Salmon or dark-meat chicken + quinoa + roasted vegetables
- **Post-training**: Lactose-free yogurt (calcium/protein) or a soy-based shake — timed away from iron-heavy meals
